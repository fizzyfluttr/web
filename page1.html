Drain De-clogger

Description

This concept is done on the  category of daily Life products. This is a design that helps to prevent the hair from clogging the drain in the baths by collecting it in it's fins. In this video you can see the working model of the product.  

Under The Fins

Under the fins there are small nozzle like vanes that captures the kinetic energy from water to keep the fins agitated.

Fin Overside

On the top, there are small cylindrical bumps  with undercuts that help the fins to catch and retain the hair.

Easy To Assemble

This Design features Plug N' Play concept. It is easy to install and very easy to take apart  and clean.
